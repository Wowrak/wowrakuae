If you find yourself in the vibrant city of Ras Al Khaimah during Ramadan, make sure to indulge in the truly ambrosial Iftar treats offered at Gazebo. Known for its delectable range of traditional Indian cuisine, this renowned restaurant never fails to impress with its culinary mastery. As you step into the elegant and inviting ambiance, your senses are immediately awakened by the fragrant aromas that fill the air - a delightful preview of what awaits on your plate. The Iftar menu is an irresistible blend of classic Arabian flavors and authentic Indian spices, expertly crafted to ensure a memorable dining experience.


From tender lamb biryanis infused with saffron and rose water to succulent chicken kebabs marinated overnight in tangy yogurt and aromatic herbs, each dish is a testament to Gazebo's commitment to quality. You will also find an array of mouthwatering vegetarian options such as paneer tikka masala and palak chole - dishes that showcase their expertise in catering to all palates. To end your meal on a sweet note, do not miss out on their heavenly homemade desserts like sheer korma or gulab jamun - decadent delights that add just the right touch o sweetness after a satisfying feast. Gazebo's attention to detail extends beyond their food; their warm hospitality ensures every guest feels valued as they break their fast together.
